What's Fleebee?

Fleebee is an all in one app where you can create, manage and organize your vacations & trips for free. Gone are those days when you had to switch between different apps and tools to manage your itenary, flight tickets, hotel reservations etc. Fleebee let's you manage everything in one place. Fleebee, based on your preferences creates a dynamic itenary which you can share with your friends/family and collaborate with them in real-time to make the best possible itenary.

FAQ

What are the benefits for a beta customer?

People who are part of the fleebee's beta phase testing process, will get 6 months of free access to all the premium features of the app once it is launched.

When will the product be launched?

The final version of the app is scheduled to be launched in August 2021.

Do I have to register to use the app?

No. Registeration is required only when you want to have access to premium features like sharing your itenary or creating a travel diary. For basic features like travel itenary it is completely free and no sigup required.

Do beta users have to register to use the app?

Only those beta users who wants to avail the 6 month access to premium features of the app after the launch are required to register.
