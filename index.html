What's Fleebee

Plan your next adventure with us

Tell us about your dream destinations, Funsafar will map a travel plan for you. You can collaborate with your tripmates and add a few more stops. We take care of your travel spending and helps create memories for life.

How it works?

Step 1. Questionnaire

Let us know your travel preference by answering few questions on your budgets, time, transport options, travel companions etc.

Step 2. 404 NOT FOUND

Yes, you read that right. There is no step 2. You will right away get your itenary after step 1. You will also have access to a wide range of features like trip expense manager, trip memorabilia etc.. which are explained below

Features

Personalized dynamic travel plan

Hassle-free dynamic itenary which keeps updating itself in real time based on the time you spend on each tourist spot..... Read More

Share and customise your trip

Share the trip with your tripmates. Add or delete your destinations to perfect your trip. We will optimize the route for you, and bring you the most.

Trip Expense Manager

Track your trip to manage trip spending using the app. Split the expenses with your tripmates and settle the balances on the go.

Manage everything in one place

Upload your flight tickets, hotel bookings etc in the app and find all those in one place inside your app. No more

Create your travel diary

Get a digital travel diary after every trip. You can share it with your friends/families and on social media. Get it printed in the form of an album for keepsake.

Nearby feature

Get information about all the nearby eateries, pharmacy, ATMs, washrooms etc at every tourist spot
